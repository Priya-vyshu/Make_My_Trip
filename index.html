Q - How do I make a flight booking on MakeMyTrip?

A: You can book a flight on MakeMyTrip in five easy steps: Head over to the MakeMyTrip flight
booking page, Enter your departure and arrival destinations, Select your air travel dates, Choose from
our wide range of cheap flights based on your airfare preferences, Click on ‘Book Now’ and your air
flight booking is done. Alternatively, you can also use the MakeMyTrip app for your flight ticket
booking. Download the MakeMyTrip app, Put in the details i.e. date of journey, departure and arrival
destinations, travel class of your choice, Select on your best comfortable option and click on 'Book
Now'.

Q - How can I avail budget air tickets on MakeMyTrip?

A: It’s super-easy to avail budget airfare while booking your cheap flight tickets on MakeMyTrip. Just
select the ‘Price’ filter once the available flight options are displayed and adjust according to your
convenience. On the MakeMyTrip app, you can select the downward arrow, which will show the
lowest airfare at the top and continue downward in ascending order.

Q - Can I avail domestic flight offers on MakeMyTrip?

A: Of course, you can. While making domestic flight bookings, you can avail any special offer that is
active at that time. In accordance with the offer selected, a listing of eligible cheapest flights would
show up on your screen. You can then apply the price filter and click on the downwards arrow,
following which budget-friendly flights would start showing up in ascending order from the top
(lowest price on top).

Q - Why could I not avail the flight booking offers at the time of
checkout?

A: MakeMyTrip makes use of a world-class real-time reservation database to list airfare and
availability. As dynamic changes in airfare take place, or the available flight tickets sell out, the
database reflects the changes in real-time. Hence, we suggest, you double-check online flight booking
prices when purchasing flight tickets, as the airfare might have been dynamically updated since you
first selected the air travel dates or planned your itinerary.
